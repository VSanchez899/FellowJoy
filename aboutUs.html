About Fellow Joy

"Fellow Joy" was started to help people in third world countries. We strive to help them with the necessities of life for example we give clothing, food, water, and in some places materials to create shelter,

Our Mission:Giving people a chance to volunteer and bring items from first world countries to those that need it.


Our Vision:Bring Joy to those people in third world countries who need it, by the donation of time and objects from people in first world countries that want to help make a difference in the world, and change the course of history.
